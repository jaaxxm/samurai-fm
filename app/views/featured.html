"Snare Force One" Radio Show w/ Jon Kennedy 12 days

Working professionally in the music industry for the last 13 years, Jon has released 6 LP's on some of the UK's best underground labels. Namely Tru-Thoughts Recordings and Manchester based Grand Central Records. He has remixed over 20 artists from Barclay James Harvest, Bonobo, Quantic, 70's Psychedelic legends Pete Brown & Piblokto and reworked the soundtrack from classic cult film 'Withnail & I'. He continues to be prolific as ever with 12 remixes being completed in 2012 alone. Touring the globe as a DJ and Live Band outfit, he visits most major city's in Europe every weekend and has reached audiences as far away as China, Japan, Lebanon, Egypt, Jordan, Malaysia, Russia and USA.

Jon's own musical productions have seen him work with Hennessey, Vans, Billabong, Quicksilver, Etnies, WEsc, DC Shoes, IVI Vision, Dulux Paints, Channel 4 (UK) and 2 independent Hollywood feature films, 1 starring Academy Award winner Olympia Dukakis, Lindsay Hollister (Get Smart) and Trevor Donovan (90210).

The future plans bring Jon to you under the umbrella of SONY MUSIC on his own record label THE JON KENNEDY FEDERATION.
