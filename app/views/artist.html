Working professionally in the music industry for the last 12 years. Jon has released 6 LP's on some of the UK's best underground labels. Namely Tru Thoughts Recordings and Manchester based Grand Central Records. He has remixed over 20 artists from Barclay James Harvest, Bob Marley, Pepe Deluxe, Bonobo, Quantic, 70's Psychedelic legends Piblokto and reworked the soundtrack from classic cult film 'Withnail & I'. He continues to be prolific as ever with 12 remixes being completed in 2012 alone. Touring the globe as a DJ and Live Band outfit, he visits most major city's in Europe every weekend and has reached audiences as far away as China, Japan, Lebanon, Egypt, Jordan, Malaysia, Russia and USA

Jon's own musical productions have seen him work with Hennessey, Vans, Billabong, DC Shoes, Etnies, WEsc, Dulux, Channel 4 (uk) and 2 Hollywood based feature films. The future brings Jon to you under the umbrella of SONY MUSIC on his own record label THE JON KENNEDY FEDERATION
